Programme

Under the tutelage of the legendary Master Chef Sanjeev Kapoor and a brigade of culinary maestros, this three-year, non-residential journey is more than a course - it's a transformation into culinary excellence. Eligibility

Ready to join the culinary brigade? Indian students need a minimum of 50% in their 12th (45% for SC/ST). International students, including NRI/PIO/Foreign Nationals, follow the SCEI norms. IB and IGCSE students, there's a place for you too, based on your board's equivalence criteria. And remember, we embrace diversity with open arms, offering reservations for SC, ST, differently-abled students, and more.

Orientation & Pedagogy

At SSCA, we believe in learning by doing. Our pedagogy is a buffet of interactive sessions, case studies, and exposure to the latest in culinary and hospitality. With state-of-the-art equipment, guest lectures from industry maestros, and field visits, we ensure you get a taste of real-world culinary challenges. Plus, get hands-on management experience in food events and outlets – it's a complete recipe for success!
